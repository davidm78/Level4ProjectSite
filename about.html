Pedometer Tracker Website


This website is designed as a companion website to an application developed as part of my 4th year student project in my Computing Science degree. The idea is that users download the app that tracks their steps over the course of the day and different time intervals, as well as locations whenever the server is hit.This information is then collated on this website and is displayed as heatmaps, total steps made by everyone, as well as additional information about each users walking habits when they log in.
